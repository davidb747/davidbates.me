About Me
Glenn Paul Gara is an Assistant Professor in Computer Science and Information Technology at the University of the Immaculate Conception. Currently, he is a Ph.D. candidate at the College of Computer Studies at De La Salle University - Manila under a scholarship grant of the Commission of Higher Education, Philippines. He is a researcher at the Center for Complexity and Emerging Technologies where he is studying the structure and dynamics of a complex network (collaboration network). Also, he is doing research and exploring the area of Machine Learning particularly on Graph Neural Networks and Geometric Deep Learning.
